Lifelike Video Conferencing

We pride ourselves in connecting people to make their workplace great. Connecting people, cutting physical barriers with world-class audio, web & video conferencing


Smarter devices for smarter collaboration.


Lifesize is a powerful video conferencing platform for meaningful meetings – whether you’re in the conference room, on the couch or somewhere in between. By connecting our innovative, easy-to-use HD cameras and touchscreen phones to our award-winning, cloud-based video conferencing service, you experience a meeting like no other. Our passion for amazing video conferencing runs deep. We did create the world’s first business-class, HD video conferencing solution, after all.

Wherever you collaborate


In Meeting Rooms

Your conference rooms are your most important meeting spaces, so let us help you treat them that way. From small huddle rooms to traditional conference rooms to large auditoriums, we have the video conference room system that’s just right (it’s the best Goldilocks scenario). Lifesize conference room systems come fully equipped with everything you need, so you can meet and breathe easy.

At Your Desk

When you need to close the loop on an endless email string or quickly catch up with colleagues across time zones, just jump on a video call with the Lifesize desktop or web app. When you want to connect with partners, customers and suppliers outside of your organization, we have a seamless guest-invite process so they can join the meeting with ease (no dial strings, passcodes or hold music).

On The Go

In school, we were always known as the one who plays well with others. Now we have a whole array of standards-based interoperability and integrations with key tools we know you use, like Skype for Business, Slack, HipChat, Outlook and so on. We even support some of those hardware investments you previously made and have thought better of since.

With Other Tools

Let’s face it — you’re busy. You often have to be in multiple places at once. Well, we can help you be there without having to be there (so you can be in the other place you really need to be). With the Lifesize mobile app, you can make and receive calls, join video conferences via video or audio-only, see what’s being shared and even use the IM chat feature to stay connected.
