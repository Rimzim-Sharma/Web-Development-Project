About Us


Business Centres Osborne Park creates better ways to empower everyone to thrive in a connected world.

Our purpose
This statement guides everything we do . Every decision, every innovation, every change.




Our brand personality

There are five pillars that inform our brand personality. Everything we do should echo these pillars.

Innovative

We’re innovators – but in an entirely different way. We transform ideas into action. And take what at first seems impossible, and make it impossible to live without.

Human

We’re about making things better for humans. We’ve moved beyond the instructional and functional, to the intuitive and natural – to make the very experience of living easier, and more enjoyable

Trusted

We want to take people places they’ve never been before – and inspire them to live in ways they’ve never dreamed of. It takes know-how, experience, and consistent excellence. And a relationship built on trust.


Imaginative

We believe that advancement starts by living in the world of what could be, rather than what is. We’re ‘imagineers’, seeking to discover something new with every step – and lighting the way to a brighter future, through just the power of our imagination.

Optimistic

We’re a company that’s always looking forward to tomorrow. We’re not shackled by expectations, or weighed down by the past – but energised by the chance to create a brighter tomorrow, for everyone.


Our brand voice

Our brand will be the personification of who we want to be, and this will come to life in how we ‘speak’.

We’re intelligent:

We deal with some of the most sophisticated and complex technology available to humans. But we communicate our technical advancements in simple, poetic language – because we know there’s nothing more clever than simplification.

We’re intuitive:

The whole point of technology is to improve people’s lives. And so every time we write about our products, services and initiatives, we’re really thinking about people. We come at subjects from a human perspective, and speak in an engaging and inspiring way that shows just how much we ‘get it’.

We’re intrepid:

We believe in doing what hasn’t been done before. We’re optimists, asking what if, seeking out new solutions, and finding inventive ways to transform our customers’ lives. It’s an attitude of courage and originality that we reflect in our words, by coming at subjects in fresh, impactful and poetic ways – no matter the subject or context.
